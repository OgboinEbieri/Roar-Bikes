About Us

Our Vision:

Roar Bikes vision is to lessen traffic by giving an adaptable,effective and clean way for the main roads and streets for short sepreate drives. We believe with the new affordable bikes, life would be easier and less stressful and you can have fun riding bikes than be stuck in traffic for hours.


We strongly believe that short drive mode can be progressively Effecient and Affordable and also guaranteeing that future ages will have a sound of situation to live.


What We Do:

We intend to supply individual vehicles which does not require petroleum derivatives. We are innovating to make a tremendous system of bikes that can be leased effectively by an easy-to-use application in pay per use plan of action. We are a mission-driven organization based out of Nigeria with a strong establishing group and has huge desires. At Roar Bikes we trust that consistently is a mission and a great achievement is so energizing when it is accomplished with fun and as a group. We also believe that with hardwork and commitment you can do anything.
